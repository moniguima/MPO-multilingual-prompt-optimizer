## Business Email Examples

### Scenario: Requesting a Project Deadline Extension

#### English (Baseline - Neutral)

**Original Prompt:**
```
I need to request an extension for the Q4 Marketing Campaign project.
The current deadline is November 15th, but due to resource constraints,
I need until November 30th. Please help me draft a professional request.
```

**Adapted Version:**
```
Subject: Request for Timeline Extension - Q4 Marketing Campaign

Hello,

I am writing to request a deadline extension for the Q4 Marketing Campaign project.

Current deadline: November 15th
Requested deadline: November 30th
Reason: Resource constraints

I would appreciate your consideration of this request.

Best regards
```

**Adaptation Notes:**
- Task-focused, direct approach
- Minimal preamble
- Standard professional tone

---

#### German (Formal - Sie)

**Adapted Version:**
```
Betreff: Antrag auf Fristverlängerung - Q4 Marketing-Kampagne

Sehr geehrte Damen und Herren,

hiermit möchte ich höflich um eine Verlängerung der Frist für das Projekt
"Q4 Marketing-Kampagne" bitten.

Aktuelle Frist: 15. November
Gewünschte Frist: 30. November
Grund: Ressourcenengpässe

Ich bitte Sie, diesen Antrag zu prüfen und mich über Ihre Entscheidung zu informieren.

Mit freundlichen Grüßen
```

**Adaptation Notes:**
- ✅ Formal greeting: "Sehr geehrte Damen und Herren"
- ✅ Uses "Sie" (formal you) throughout
- ✅ Structured format with clear sections
- ✅ Polite request framing: "höflich bitten"
- ✅ Professional closing: "Mit freundlichen Grüßen"
- ✅ High directness maintained (German preference)

**Cultural Context:**
German business communication values clarity, structure, and formality in professional contexts.
The "Sie" form shows respect and maintains professional distance.

---

#### Spanish (Formal - Usted)

**Adapted Version:**
```
Asunto: Solicitud de Ampliación de Plazo - Campaña de Marketing Q4

Estimados señores,

Espero que se encuentren bien.

Me dirijo a ustedes para solicitar respetuosamente una extensión del plazo
para el proyecto "Campaña de Marketing Q4".

Plazo actual: 15 de noviembre
Plazo solicitado: 30 de noviembre
Motivo: Limitaciones de recursos

Agradecería mucho su consideración de esta solicitud.

Quedo a la espera de su respuesta.

Atentamente
```

**Adaptation Notes:**
- ✅ Formal greeting: "Estimados señores"
- ✅ Warm opening: "Espero que se encuentren bien"
- ✅ Uses "usted" form throughout
- ✅ Respectful request framing: "solicitar respetuosamente"
- ✅ Gratitude expression: "Agradecería mucho"
- ✅ Relationship-acknowledging closing
- ✅ Formal closing: "Atentamente"

**Cultural Context:**
Spanish business communication emphasizes relationship-building and warmth even in formal contexts.
The well-being inquiry and gratitude expressions build rapport.

## Technical Explanation Examples

### Scenario: Explaining RAG Architecture to Executives

#### English (Neutral)

**Original Prompt:**
```
Explain RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture to non-technical executives.
Keep it simple and focus on business value.
```

**Adapted Version:**
```
RAG combines a knowledge database with AI generation:

1. **Retrieval**: System searches relevant documents
2. **Augmentation**: Adds context to the AI prompt
3. **Generation**: AI produces informed responses

Business Value:
- More accurate answers
- Reduces hallucinations
- Leverages existing company knowledge
```

---

#### German (Formal)

**Adapted Version:**
```
Sehr geehrte Damen und Herren,

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) ist eine Architektur, die folgende
Komponenten kombiniert:

1. **Abruf**: Das System durchsucht relevante Dokumente
2. **Erweiterung**: Kontext wird zur KI-Eingabe hinzugefügt
3. **Generierung**: Die KI erstellt fundierte Antworten

Geschäftlicher Nutzen:
- Präzisere Antworten
- Reduzierung von Fehlinformationen
- Nutzung vorhandenen Unternehmenswissens

Mit freundlichen Grüßen
```

**Adaptation Notes:**
- Structured, numbered format (German preference)
- Technical precision maintained
- Clear section headers

## Creative Writing Examples

### Scenario: Mystery Novel Opening

#### English (Casual)

**Original Prompt:**
```
Write the opening paragraph of a mystery novel set in a foggy coastal town.
```

**Adapted Version:**
```
The fog rolled in like a gray blanket, thick and cold, swallowing
the lighthouse beam before it could reach the shore. Detective Sarah Chen
stood at the cliff's edge, her coat whipping in the wind, staring down at
the body they'd found at dawn. The town had seen its share of accidents,
but this—this was something different.
```

---

#### Spanish (Neutral)

**Adapted Version:**
```
La niebla llegó como una manta gris, espesa y fría, devorando la luz
del faro antes de que pudiera alcanzar la costa. La detective Sarah Chen
permanecía en el borde del acantilado, su abrigo agitándose con el viento,
mirando hacia abajo al cuerpo que habían encontrado al amanecer. El pueblo
había visto su parte de accidentes, pero esto—esto era algo diferente.
```

**Adaptation Notes:**
- Maintains atmospheric tone
- Natural Spanish narrative flow
- Culturally appropriate descriptive language

## Key Adaptation Patterns

### Formality Markers

| Language | Casual | Neutral | Formal |
|----------|--------|---------|--------|
| **German** | du | Sie (context) | Sie + titles |
| **Spanish** | tú | usted (mixed) | usted + titles |
| **English** | Hey/Hi | Hello | Dear Sir/Madam |

### Opening Patterns

| Language | Formality | Opening Example |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **German** | Formal | "Sehr geehrte Damen und Herren," |
| **German** | Casual | "Hallo," / "Hi," |
| **Spanish** | Formal | "Estimados señores," + "Espero que se encuentren bien." |
| **Spanish** | Casual | "Hola," + "¿Cómo estás?" |
| **English** | Formal | "Dear Sir/Madam," |
| **English** | Casual | "Hi there," |

### Closing Patterns

| Language | Formality | Closing Example |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **German** | Formal | "Mit freundlichen Grüßen," |
| **German** | Casual | "Liebe Grüße," / "Viele Grüße," |
| **Spanish** | Formal | "Atentamente," / "Cordialmente," |
| **Spanish** | Casual | "Saludos," / "Un abrazo," |
| **English** | Formal | "Best regards," / "Sincerely," |
| **English** | Casual | "Cheers," / "Thanks," |

### Request Framing

| Language | Style | Example |
|----------|-------|---------|
| **German** | Direct | "Ich bitte Sie, [request]" |
| **Spanish** | Respectful | "Me gustaría solicitar respetuosamente [request]" |
| **English** | Neutral | "I would like to request [request]" |
